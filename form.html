Spay/Neuter Services

Spay/neuter services are available for cats and kittens that are at least 3 pounds and 3 months of age. On the morning of surgery, our veterinarian will clear your cat by performing a brief examination. If your cat is deemed overweight, our vet will use his discretion on whether we can go forward for surgery or if your cat needs to lose weight before he or she can have surgery.

State law requires that all companion animals be up-to-date on rabies vaccines. If your cat or kitten is up-to-date, please bring his/her rabies certificate with you on the morning of surgery or have your veterinarian email or fax the rabies certificate to us. If your cat or kitten is not up-to-date, we can vaccinate him/her the same day as surgery.

We understand that for reasons beyond your control, you might miss your appointment. We are happy to reschedule you. We encourage you to commit to your appointment. If you decide to cancel your appointment for any reason, please let us know 24 hours in advance. Thank you.
